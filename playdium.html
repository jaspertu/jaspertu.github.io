    What is Playdium?

    Grade 6 to 8 kids enjoy being physically connected through fun, outdoor games. However, this positive experience has now been taken away due to COVID-19. From being locked up at home, user research revealed 11–13 year olds often suffer from increased anxiety, nightmares, and meltdowns. Hence, we digitized manhunt—one of their favourite games—to let 11–13 year olds spark joy, relieve stress, and enlighten them on best health practices.

    Playdium cheers up lonely children stuck at home by letting them personalize their own character, theme their game worlds with Nickelodeon shows, and most importantly, connect with friends and family through manhunt within a rich, 3D world. Simultaneously, it facilitates learning of important health and safety knowledge pertaining to COVID-19; if one gets “tagged”, they are challenged to answer questions correctly within a time limit. Enriching the game experience are items which give temporary power-ups.
