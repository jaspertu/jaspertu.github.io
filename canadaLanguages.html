    Canada's Languages

    A website exploring Canada's top non-official languages

    Jasper Tu • 1 min read

    Background

    I spotted an opportunity to make an informative website for tourists vacationing in Canada. This is because since Canada is considered a cultural mosaic, I wanted to use its diversity to both attract and enlighten individuals who may be unfamiliar with Canadian culture. Thus, the most spoken non-official languages were the focal point of my content.

    Objective

    Fusing data visualization and a fun visual identity to create an exciting website for tourists.
